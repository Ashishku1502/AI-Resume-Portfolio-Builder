## üîß Troubleshooting

### Problem: No text extracted
**Solution**: Your PDF may be image-based or encrypted. Try:
1. Converting PDF to TXT format
2. Copy-pasting text manually into form fields
3. Using a different PDF viewer to export as text

### Problem: Partial text extracted
**Solution**: Your PDF may have a complex layout. Try:
1. Reviewing extracted text and filling in gaps
2. Using manual input for missing sections
3. Converting to a simpler PDF format

### Problem: Garbled text
**Solution**: Your PDF may use special encoding. Try:
1. Re-saving PDF with standard fonts
2. Converting to TXT format
3. Using manual input
